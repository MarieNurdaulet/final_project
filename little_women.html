Released December 25, 2019

Stars

Saoirse Ronan, Florence Pugh, Emma Watson, Eliza Scanlen, Timothée Chalamet, Laura Dern

Genre

Drama, Historical Fiction

What I Have Learned

"Little Women" teaches the value of staying true to yourself while navigating societal expectations. As someone from a small town, it resonates deeply—showing how dreams can take you far but home always shapes who you are. Jo’s determination reminds me that ambition doesn’t have to compromise kindness. Amy’s journey teaches that refinement and ambition can coexist, and Beth’s quiet strength shows that a fulfilling life doesn’t always mean a loud one. Above all, it emphasizes the power of sisterhood, community, and finding joy in small, everyday moments.
