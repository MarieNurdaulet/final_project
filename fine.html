What I Have Learned

Fine on the Outside by Priscilla Ahn is a hauntingly beautiful song featured in Studio Ghibli's When Marnie Was There. The song reflects themes of loneliness, emotional vulnerability, and self-discovery, resonating deeply with the film's protagonist. Ahn's gentle, introspective vocals evoke a sense of quiet sadness and introspection, while the lyrics mirror the film's exploration of identity, friendship, and the healing power of connection. The track beautifully enhances the film’s atmosphere, leaving listeners with a lingering sense of emotional depth and introspection.
