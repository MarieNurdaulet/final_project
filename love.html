First Love

Released November 24, 2022

Stars

Hikari Mitsushima, Takeru Satoh, Rikako Yagi, Taisei Kido, Kaho

Genre

Romance, melodrama

What I Have Learned

First Love: Hatsukoi portrays the enduring impact of one's first love, illustrating how early relationships shape our lives and memories. It delves into themes of nostalgia, personal growth, and the passage of time, highlighting the bittersweet nature of love and the importance of cherishing meaningful connections. The series encourages reflection on past experiences and their influence on our present selves.
