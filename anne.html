Anne with an E

Released May 12, 2017

Stars

Amybeth McNulty, Geraldine James, R.H. Thomson, Dalila Bela, Lucas Jade Zumann

Genre

Period drama, coming-of-age, family

What I Have Learned

Anne with an E showcases the power of imagination and resilience in the face of adversity. It emphasizes the importance of belonging and love while challenging societal norms around feminism and equality. The series also highlights the value of friendship and loyalty, portraying relationships that inspire and support personal growth..
